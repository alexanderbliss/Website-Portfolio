BounceTribe

React, Relay, GraphQL


With a new generation of musicians using technology to record and produce music in their bedrooms, the process of making music has never been easier. However, it's also easy to feel alone without the input of others.

The goal of BounceTribe is to connect musicians and encourage the exchange of ideas and suggestions to help songwriters improve their songs and increase their confidence.



BounceTribe brought me need as a Web Developer. BounceTribe is built with React, Relay, and GraphQL. I am currently working on developing features for BounceTribe. More work to come...

In mean time you can visit the website and join the tribe for free today at www.bouncetribe.com.
