STEP-UP Check-In

AngularJS, Node.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL


STEP-UP is part of the City of Minneapolis achievempls program. They train and place hundreds of Minneapolis youth in paid internships with top Twin Cities employers. STEP-UP approached me and other developers to design and develop a full-stack application called STEP-UP Check-In to reach out to over 1700 interns in their summer internship.



STEP-UP Check-In is built with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap, AngularJS, Express.js, Node.js, Google OAuth, Twilio, and Heroku.

STEP-UP wanted admins and interns to log in to the application using their Gmail.



Admins are able to upload a .csv file containing all interns info(name, phone, Gmail, and internship). They are able to edit/delete intern's information if need be.



At the end of the work week, admins can create questions and add them to the database. They can mark a question as flagged, meaning that if an intern's response is negative they will be alerted. Admins can create the weekly feedback form by selecting three questions from a list of questions with a scale of 1 - 5. Once the feedback form is created admins can send a link to the form by text message to +1700 interns with a single click of a button.



Once the work week has ended, interns will receive a text message from STEP-UP prompting them to give feedback about their week. Interns can fill the feedback form on their mobile devices making it easy and user-friendly. At the bottom of the feedback form, interns can check a box to let admins at STEP-UP know that they would like someone to contact them. Interns can also leave a comment if they want. Once completing the feedback interns will submit the form for the week.



At the beginning of a new work week, admins can see an which interns want to be contacted, flagged interns, and comments interns left. Admins can see a graphical overview data of all the responses interns gave.



A super admin can add, delete, and edit new admins/staff to STEP-UP Check-In.



Conclusion

STEP-UP Check-In is a culmination of my skills, as a Full-Stack Developer. Designing and developing an application that is responsive and user-friendly for STEP-UP to receive intern's feedback about their summer internship.

To learn more about achievempls and their STEP-UP program visit www.achievempls.org
